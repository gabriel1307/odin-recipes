Chihiro's Onigiri

Recipe of the snack that bought tears to Chihiro's eyes

“They don't remember being human, so look hard. It's up to you to remember which ones they are.”

Ingredients:

  • 6 mini sheets of roasted seaweed (aka "nori")
  • 3/4 cup shredded cooked salmon
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 6 cups cooked sticky rice
  • a triangle shaped cookie cutter

Directions:

  1. Slice the nori sheets
  2. Mix the mayonnaise into your salmon until it's well coated
  3. Put the cookie cutter on a flat surface. Place a sheet of plastic wrap over the cookie cutter and push some of it down inside so that the inside of the cookie cutter is completely lined with plastic wrap.
  4. Scoop approximately 2 tbsp of rice into the bottom of the cutter and press it flat with a plastic spoon or small rubber spatula
  5. Place 1 tsp salmon filling into the middle of the rice, spreading it around just a little,
  6. Place a couple more tablespoons of rice on top and flatten out as before. Add a little extra rice if you need more to cover up the salmon.
  8. Wrap the plastic tightly over the exposed end of the onigiri and pop it out of the cookie cutter. Firmly wrap any loose ends around the outside of the onigiri.
  9. Repeat with remaining rice and salmon until you run out of ingredients
  10. Let the onigiri set for 5-10 minutes. If planning to eat them later, place them on a baking sheet and chill until ready to serve
  11. When ready to serve, remove the plastic wrap and press a nori strip around the bottom of each onigiri.
