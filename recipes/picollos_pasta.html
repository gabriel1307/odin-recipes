Piccolo's Pasta!

Recipe of the delicious pasta served in Studio Ghibli's "Porco Rosso" Movie.

Time: 45-60min

Serving: 4 people

Ingredients:

  • Oil
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 large white onion diced
  • 2-3 garlic cloves
  • Salt & pepper
  • 2-3 bayleafs
  • 1/2 tbsp of italian spices
  • 1/2 tbsp of sugar
  • 1/2 lb of spaghetti
  • Fresh sweet basil (optional)
